Details of the State Conference

NCCF is a Christian Organization in Nigeria that brings together corps members from across the federation, spreading the gospel of our Lord Jesus Christ. It has been our custom from time memorial to organize Prayer/State Conferences every year. By God's grace we will be having our State Conference which is an event that brings together the Christian Youth Corps Members from across the zones/local government areas in the state and beyond to rekindle our fire of prayer, passion for the gospel and targeted at equipping and building young people. The Theme for this year's State Conference : LIVING WATER (John 7:38; Jer 17:13; Rev 22:17; John 4:10;) which starts from Thursday 6th - Sunday 9th of May 2021 @ Kathryn M. Dick Memorial Youth Center (Nigerian Youth Camp) Eyekonrin, Ilorin, along Airport Road. Conference opens at 12 noon (arrival) on Thursday.
